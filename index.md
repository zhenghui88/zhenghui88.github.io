## About Me

I am a Senior Engineer at the [Institute of Atmospheric Physics](http://www.iap.cas.cn/), [Chinese Academy of Sciences](http://www.cas.cn/).

My research focuses on:

- Regional Earth system model development;
- Physics-data integration;
- High-resolution hindcasting, nowcasting, and forecasting of droughts and floods over large domains.
